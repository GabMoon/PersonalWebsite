Determined, adaptable, and goal-driven Software Developer with a passion for data and varied experience writing relevant code tied to real-world business applications.

When I started college I thought that being successful required you to bite the bullet and get a career that was 'serious' and not necessarily fun. I chose an Accounting major because it was the 'smart' choice, despite promising myself when I was younger that no matter what I would find a career that let me create and shape my work product. While I found a lot to love about accounting and the satisfaction of well-organized data, there is very little creativity permitted in accounting.

I didn't hit my stride until a required computer basics course that introduced me to programming. Suddenly I was looking at all of these business softwares I worked with from a whole new angle. It inspired me to imagine new, more efficient ways of organizing my workflow. Inspired me to recommit to a career that embraced innovation and change. My experiences led me to become the Software Developer I am today.
